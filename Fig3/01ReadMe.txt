This directory contains everything to create Fig 3.

######################################
Underlying data:

./Data/ParticleTrajectoryDiscArena.txt

Data file contain trajectory of the sqigglebot in disc arena.
Content of data file: 1st column x position, 2nd column y position.
Frames (individual positions) are recorded at a rate of 25 frames per second.

######################################
The python script 02_ComputeAngle.py evaluate the data and generate curves for Fig3.

- identifies bulk trajectories (starting at boundary, ending at boundary) from the total trajectory of the sqigglebot
- computes both, incoming and outgoing angle for each bulk trajectory
- creates the histogram (probability distribution) of incoming and outgoing angles of the ball in disc arena
- provides all quantities necessary to show two sample bulk trajectories and their incoming and outgoing angles


-------> To compute outgoing angle launch script as <-------
python3 02_ComputeAngle.py out
- computes outgoing angle of bulk trajectories

-------> To compute incoming angle launch script as <-------
python3 02_ComputeAngle.py in
- computes outgoing angle of TIME-REVERSED bulk trajectories, i.e the incoming angle of the NON-time-reversed bulk trajectories


Input : ./Data/ParticleTrajectoryDiscArena.txt

Output :

'./Data/F3b_ProbOfInAngle.txt' for (python3 02_ComputeAngle.py in)
1st column : incoming angle
2nd column : normalised probability of incoming angle

'./Data/F3b_ProbOfOutAngle.txt' for (python3 02_ComputeAngle.py out)
1st column : outgoing angle
2nd column : normalised probability of outgoing angle

'./ProbOfIn.png' for (python3 02_ComputeAngle.py in)
Figure of probability of incoming angle

'./ProbOfOut.png' for (python3 02_ComputeAngle.py out)
Figure of probability of outgoing angle

The following files contain data for visualisation of incoming and outgoing angles used for Fig. 3a:
	'./Data/F3a_Parameters.txt'
	'./Data/F3a_InVectors_ID*.txt'
	'./Data/F3a_Trajectory_ID*.txt'
	'./Data/F3a_OutVectors_ID*.txt'
	'./Data/TrajectoryInDisk.txt'

'TimeForward*.png' and 'TimeReversed*.png':
	visualisation of some bulk trajectories
